Street Art Workshop Tour in Valparaiso

Price: USD $ 125

SKU: 006_w

This is the opportunity to be a graffiti artist for a day and keep practicing forever

Categories: All Tours, Arty, Street Art

Tags: art, graffiti, walking, workshop

This is a perfect option for a hands-on approach, as you will be submerged into the sub-culture of street art.

We begin by going to a place where you will create your tag name and draw a couple of ideas that you will eventually put on the wall. The idea is to understand either the method to draw letters or characters, and eventually turn them into a piece of street art. We will then head over to the wall, where our instructors will teach you all about the use of “caps”, “pressure levels”, and techniques to make your work come alive.

You’ll also be able to take home an original piece of work with you!
