Bike and Street Art Tour in Valparaíso

Price: USD $ 95

SKU: 006_b

A downhill bike tour, where you'll be able to take in amazing sights and great works of art

Categories: All Tours, Arty, Cycling Tours, Street Art

Tags: art, cycling, graffiti, walking

A downhill bike tour, where you’ll be able to take in amazing sights and great works of art. You’ll also visit Neruda’s House, and take home an original Street Art print.

This tour was designed as a scenic option to enjoy a bike ride, though Valparaiso’s hills. We begin this tour at one of the higher points of Valparaiso, which is Plazuela San Luis. Here you will enjoy of a nice and easy ride along Avenida Alemania.  We then visit a very important place for Street Art, Pablo Neruda’s house. Though we don’t enter the house, we do visit the gardens and enjoy of the great view offered by this place.

We continue downhill to see the works of BRP, UnKolorDistinto, Inti, amongst many more artists.

Along the route, you will also meet a local artist and get a first hand impression on the motives behind their work, and what drives them to take part in this interesting street culture scene.

You’ll also be able to take home an original piece of work with you!
