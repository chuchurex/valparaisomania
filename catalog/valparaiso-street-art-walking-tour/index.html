Valparaiso Street Art Walking Tour

Price: USD $ 35

SKU: 006_w

Graffiti Treasure hunting through Valparaíso labyrinthine streets and hidden alleys

Categories: All Tours, Arty, Street Art

Tags: art, graffiti, walking

Learn about the amazing works that are found in Chile’s street art Capital. Your expert guide will take you through this port city and show you the hidden gems found in Valparaiso.

We begin this tour by picking you up at your accommodation in Valparaiso. From there we will head over to the Anibal Pinto square in order to begin our route. Our intention here is to show you how graffiti evolves into marvelous works of art, which is why we begin explaining the vandalism aspect of graffiti. As we make our way though the narrow streets and staircases covered  with colors and stories, you will discover the techniques and intentions behind these works.

Along the route, you will also meet a local artist and get a first hand impression on the motives behind their work, and what drives them to take part in this interesting street culture scene.

You’ll also be able to take home an original piece of work with you!
