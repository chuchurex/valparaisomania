ABOUT VALPARAISOMANIA

In Valparaisomania.com we seek, curate and select the most memorable travel experiences in the region of Valparaiso in Chile. More than tours, we offer unique experiences delivered by local people with a great cultural stamp. A good tour is not always about travelling in the best minivan, it’s about memories, real people and local culture.

We take great pride in customizing our tours to suit you, focusing on whatever might interest you the most. Classic tours and less conventional ones, cooking classes, sailing, visiting markets, ecotaxis, wine tastings…you name it! Our guides are highly qualified, versatile, friendly and fun.

Select one of the listed services or ask us to organize the kind of tour that you like, easily paid online with Paypal.
